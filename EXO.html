EXO!
WE ARE ONE

Exo is a South Korean-Chinese boy band based in Seoul, with nine members: Xiumin, Suho,Lay, Baekhyun, Chen, Chanyeol, D.O., Kai and Sehun. The band was formed by SM Entertainment in 2011 and debuted in 2012. Their music incorporates genres like pop, hip-hop, and R&B.
